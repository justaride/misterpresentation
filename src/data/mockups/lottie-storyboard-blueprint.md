# Presentation Blueprint: User Journey – The Happy Path

## Slide 1 – The Beginning of the Journey
**Purpose:** Introduce the user and their initial need or pain point.

**Content:**
**Headline (Top-Center):**
"Meet Alex."
**Body Text (Bottom-Center):**
"Alex has a problem. A cluttered inbox and a deadline that won't wait. They need a solution that feels like a breath of fresh air."

**Layout:**
- Large central area for animation.
- Headline at the top, body text at the bottom.
- Centered horizontally.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Outfit", Bold, 64pt, Indigo-900 (#1E1B4B).
- Body Text: "Outfit", Regular, 24pt, Slate-600 (#475569).

**Visual:**
- A Lottie animation of a character looking overwhelmed at a desk with flying papers and notification icons.
- Style: Flat, modern, colorful.
- Colors: Indigo, Teal, and Coral accents.
