# Presentation Blueprint: Corporate Synergy Q3 Review

## Slide 1 – Title Slide
**Purpose:** Set the formal, slightly sterile corporate tone.

**Content:**
**Headline (Center, Large):**
"Q3 Strategic Performance Review"
**Subheading (Center, Medium):**
"Synergy, Optimization, and Paradigm Shifts"
**Presenter Info (Bottom-Right, Small):**
"J. P. Buzzword, Senior VP of Cloud Synergy"

**Layout:**
- Centered headline and subheading.
- Presenter info in bottom-right corner.
- Generous white space.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Source Sans Pro", Bold, 72pt, Dark Blue (#1A365D).
- Subheading: "Source Sans Pro", Light, 32pt, Gray (#4A5568).
- Presenter Info: "Source Sans Pro", Italic, 16pt, Gray (#718096).

**Visual:**
- A very subtle geometric pattern in the background (light gray lines).
- Background: Off-white (#F7FAFC).

---

## Slide 2 – Our Mission (Vertical Slide 2.1)
**Purpose:** Reiterate corporate platitudes.

**Content:**
**Headline (Top-Center):**
"Our Core Competencies"
**Bullet Points (Center):**
- Leveraging actionable analytics
- Driving ecosystemic growth
- Incentivizing proactive disruption

**Layout:**
- Headline at top.
- Centered list.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Source Sans Pro", Bold, 48pt, Dark Blue.
- List Items: "Source Sans Pro", Regular, 32pt, Dark Blue.

**Visual:**
- Three circular icons next to each bullet point (Network, Growth, Lightning). Simple line art.

---

## Slide 3 – The Numbers (Vertical Slide 2.2)
**Purpose:** Show "growth" using abstract charts.

**Content:**
**Headline (Top-Left):**
"Up and To The Right"
**Chart Legend (Right):**
- Blue: Revenue
- Green: Synergy
- Red: Lunch Costs

**Layout:**
- Headline top-left.
- Large area for chart.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Source Sans Pro", Bold, 48pt, Dark Blue.
- Legend: "Source Sans Pro", Semibold, 24pt, Dark Blue.

**Visual:**
- A line chart with three lines, all trending upwards significantly.
- "Revenue" line has a small rocket icon at the end.

---

## Slide 4 – The "Pivot"

**Content:**

"THE PIVOT"
**Subheading (Center, Medium):**
"Why we are now a Coffee-as-a-Service Company"

**Layout:**
- Centered.

**Typography:**

## Slide 5 – Global Reach
**Purpose:** Show international scale.

**Content:**
**Headline (Top-Center):**
"Global Footprint"
**Map Callouts:**
- London: "Strategic Tea Supply"
- Tokyo: "Advanced Matcha R&D"
- New York: "Espresso Optimization"

**Layout:**
- Headline at top.
- Map visual takes up 80% of the slide.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Source Sans Pro", Bold, 48pt, White.
- Callouts: "Source Sans Pro", Semibold, 18pt, White.

**Visual:**
- A stylized world map (dotted pattern).
- Glowing points on specific cities.
