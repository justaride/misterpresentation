# Presentation Blueprint: The Deep Sea Expedition

## Slide 1 – The Surface
**Purpose:** Begin the journey at sea level, introducing the concept of descent.

**Content:**
**Headline (Center, Floating):**
"THE DESCENT"
**Subheading (Center, Below Headline):**
"A journey to the bottom of the world."
**Depth Marker (Right, Vertical):**
"0m"

**Layout:**
- Centered title.
- Depth marker fixed on the right side.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Cinzel", Bold, 80pt, White (with soft blue glow).
- Subheading: "Raleway", Light, 32pt, Cyan-100.
- Depth Marker: "Inconsolata", Regular, 16pt, White/50%.

**Visual:**
- Background: Bright, sunlit ocean surface (Light Blue #00A8E8 to #007EA7).
- Visual Elements: Clouds drifting slowly (Parallax Layer 1). Waves undulating (Parallax Layer 2).
- Sun rays penetrating the water from the top.

---

## Slide 2 – The Twilight Zone (200m)
**Purpose:** Transition to the region where sunlight begins to fade.

**Content:**
**Headline (Left):**
"The Twilight Zone"
**Body Text (Left):**
"Where sunlight struggles to reach.
Photosynthesis stops here.
The water turns colder."
**Depth Marker (Right):**
"200m"

**Layout:**
- Text on the left.
- Open water on the right.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Cinzel", Bold, 48pt, White.
- Body Text: "Raleway", Regular, 24pt, Cyan-200.

**Visual:**
- Background: Deepening Blue (#003459).
- Visual Elements: Schools of small fish silhouettes swimming past (Parallax Layer 3).
- Light is significantly dimmer.

---

## Slide 3 – The Midnight Zone (1,000m)
**Purpose:** Enter total darkness. Introduce bioluminescence.

**Content:**
**Headline (Center):**
"The Midnight Zone"
**Quote (Center):**
"The only light is the light we make."
**Depth Marker (Right):**
"1,000m"

**Layout:**
- Centered.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Cinzel", Bold, 48pt, White.
- Quote: "Raleway", Italic, 32pt, Bioluminescent Green (#00FF9D).

**Visual:**
- Background: Pitch Black / Very Dark Blue (#00171F).
- Visual Elements: Floating bioluminescent jellyfish (Parallax Layer 4).
- Anglerfish silhouette drifting in the distance.

---

## Slide 4 – The Abyss (4,000m)
**Purpose:** Highlight the extreme pressure and desolation.

**Content:**
**Headline (Right):**
"The Abyss"
**Body Text (Right):**
"Crushing pressure.
Near freezing temperatures.
Yet, life finds a way."
**Depth Marker (Right):**
"4,000m"

**Layout:**
- Text aligned to the right.

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Cinzel", Bold, 48pt, White.
- Body Text: "Raleway", Regular, 24pt, Gray-400.

**Visual:**
- Background: Absolute Void Black (#000000).
- Visual Elements: Giant Squid tentacle passing by in the foreground (blurred, Parallax Layer 5).
- "Marine Snow" (particles) falling slowly.

---

## Slide 5 – The Trenches (10,000m)
**Purpose:** Reach the bottom. The ultimate frontier.

**Content:**
**Headline (Center):**
"Challenger Deep"
**Subheading (Center):**
"10,935 meters down."
**Footer (Bottom):**
"We have touched the bottom."

**Layout:**
- Centered text, anchored to the "floor".

**Typography:**
- Headline: "Cinzel", Black, 64pt, White.
- Subheading: "Raleway", Bold, 32pt, Red-500 (Warning color).

**Visual:**
- Background: Black.
- Visual Elements: The ocean floor rising up (Parallax Layer 6).
- A robotic submersible resting on the sand.
- Dust clouds settling.
