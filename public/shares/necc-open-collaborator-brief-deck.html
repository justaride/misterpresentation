NECC: Building the Nordic Circular Construction Engine

A short briefing on what this project wants to achieve, how the platform works, and the impact it can create for circular construction in the Nordics.

Nordic Ecosystem for Circular Construction Short deck: 7-8 minutes Date: 12 Feb 2026

Challenge and Timing

Why this matters now

Core market frictions

Circular intent is rising, execution is fragmented

  • Supply-demand disconnection: buyers and suppliers still struggle to find each other at Nordic scale.
  • Low trust and weak comparability: circular options are often harder to assess against linear alternatives.
  • Regulatory and language fragmentation: cross-border scaling remains operationally heavy.
  • Financing complexity: teams lack a single place to navigate relevant funding and incentives.

Expected policy pressure

23-24%

EU-aligned circularity level expected in construction by 2030, with 2026 as a critical policy year.

Current Nordic baseline

5-8%

Current circularity range in Nordic construction and property value chains.

Technical upside

50-95%

Documented technical circularity potential in building projects, depending on project type.

Strategic window

Now

This is a narrow timing window to build practical infrastructure that helps circular solutions become standard choices.

Platform Blueprint

What we are building: a market accelerator platform

Demand side

Property owners and project clients KLP, Entra, Veidekke, AF Gruppen, municipalities, and public procurement teams.
High-need buyers Organizations under ESG and CSRD pressure with immediate circular sourcing gaps.
Key market criteria Affordability, availability, and profitability must all be solved simultaneously.

NECC Core

Market (Innovation) Accelerator Platform

  • Catalog of 50-100 circular solutions in construction
  • AI search (Go Circular) matched to project context
  • Funding opportunity map and deal pathways
  • Regulatory guidance across Nordic jurisdictions
  • Value-chain intelligence: who can deliver what, where, and when
  • Built on pre-project and proof-of-concept work from Public Circular Buildings
Affordability Availability Profitability

Supply side

Circular products and services Material reuse, engineered timber, upgraded steel, low-carbon and circular components.
Examples already mapped Resirqel, Omtre, Nordic Circles, Brukspecialisten, Recoma, Naeste.
External ecosystem partners Nordic Circular Hotspot (owner), Natural State (host), plus partner network integrations.

User Flow

How it works in a real project journey

"I need circular facade and interior solutions for a 4-floor project in Western Sweden. Show me viable suppliers, regulation constraints, and funding paths."

Step 1

Project brief input

Location, building type, timeline, material preferences, and circular ambition level.

Step 2

AI-assisted search

Go Circular identifies candidate products and suppliers by fit, geography, and readiness.

Step 3

Decision support

Comparable options with circular attributes, availability indicators, and advisory context.

Step 4

Regulation and funding

Country-specific guidance plus relevant grants, public schemes, and financing opportunities.

Step 5

Action and matchmaking

Buyer-supplier connection, pilot activation, and progress tracked through platform analytics.

Technical Confidence

Execution model: phased build, grounded in proven pre-project work

Phase 1 | Foundation M1-M6: schema adaptation, catalog MVP, basic search.
Phase 2 | Integrations M4-M12: supplier onboarding, Nordic data coverage, API links.
Phase 3 | AI and features M12-M24: advisory, matching, reporting, paid features.
Phase 4 | Scale M24-M36: optimization, expansion, self-sustaining operations.
Experience layer Next.js App Router UI for buyers, suppliers, and partners.
Integration layer API gateway for registry, statistics, and ecosystem data sources.
Data layer PostgreSQL core, full-text search baseline, structured product catalog.
Intelligence layer AI parsing, matching, explainable recommendations, report generation.
